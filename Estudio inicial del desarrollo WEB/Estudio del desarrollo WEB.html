Párrafo 1

Though she is a viable unit without any further investment, Layla’s first Constellation boasts an overall increase in her abilities that is impossible to ignore. As a result, any meta team or build should operate with a C1 Layla at the minimum.

Párrafo 2

The core of Layla’s kit is her Elemental Skill, Nights of Formal Focus; this provides a shield of Cryo around the members of your party that’s power scales from Layla’s Max HP. This skill also generates Night Stars whenever a character within the shield uses their Elemental Skill. Once four have been generated, they will fire off as projectiles, causing damage and applying Cryo to the target.

Párrafo 3

The reason Layla’s first Constellation is so essential is twofold; not only does it provide a hefty 20 percent increase to the Shield Absorption stat of Nights of Formal Focus (which allows it to take more damage before dissipating), it also means that whenever Layla uses her skill during co-op play, any nearby party members will also be covered by a Cryo shield.

Párrafo 4

As it is the first unlock of Layla’s Constellation, it is a highly accessible buff that allows Layla to provide better support for the party while playing alone or during co-op.
